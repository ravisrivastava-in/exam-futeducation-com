    Common Mistakes to Avoid

    Many students face counselling issues due to small errors during registration. Ensure you avoid:

    Name Mismatch: Name must match exactly with Class 10th Marksheet and Aadhaar across all exam forms.
    Wrong Category: Selecting OBC/EWS without having a valid central certificate leads to disqualification.
    Blurred Uploads: Uploading unclear photos or signatures leads to form rejection by exam authority.
    Missing Confirmation: Forgetting to download the final confirmation page after fee payment.
